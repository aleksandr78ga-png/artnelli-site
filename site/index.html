Important before booking
15,000 RUB

The 15,000 RUB deposit is paid when reserving a tailoring slot at the Nelli atelier and is fully included in the final costume price.

  • The deposit is non-refundable once work on your sketches has begun.
  • In the request, specify the discipline and desired completion date if there is an important competition.
  • The selected month is the month when the atelier starts working on the sketches.
  • After sketch approval and measurements, tailoring takes approximately 3–4 weeks.
  • Nelli will contact you personally in WhatsApp when it is time to begin the sketches.

Frequently asked questions

(06) — FAQ
Can I order a rhythmic gymnastics leotard remotely?

Yes. We discuss the idea, sketch and materials remotely, and you can take measurements at home using our step-by-step guide.

Are ready-made leotards available?

Yes. The website includes new and pre-owned models. Availability follows the atelier’s product listings in Telegram.

How do I take measurements for a leotard?

Use the separate PDF guide for a girl or a boy. The diagrams show height, body measurements and body girth.

Where does the atelier deliver?

We work with clients in different countries. The delivery option and timing are confirmed individually for each order.
